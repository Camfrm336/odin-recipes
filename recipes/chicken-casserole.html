Chicken Casserole

This Chicken Broccoli Rice Casserole includes seasoned chicken, fresh broccoli, and converted rice mixed together with a sour cream and mayonnaise mixture, then topped with two kinds of cheese and buttery breadcrumbs. Cooked up all in one pan, so clean up is a breeze! Easy and delicious weeknight dinner.

Ingredients:

  • Broccoli
  • Chicken
  • Converted rice
  • Sharp cheddar cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Sour cream, Mayonnaise, and Spicy Brown Mustard
  • Worcestershire sauce and Sriracha hot sauce
  • Panko bread crumbs, lightly toasted
  • Seasonings
  • Butter,melted

Steps:

  1. Cook the broccoli with water in a skillet for a few minutes until crisp-tender. Drain and wipe the skillet dry.

  2. Combine all of your sauce ingredients in a large bowl.

  3. Stir in the cooked broccoli, chicken, cooked rice, and 1/2 the cheddar cheese.

  4. Spoon the mixture into the skillet you used to cook the broccoli.

  5. Loosely cover with foil and bake until bubbly.

  6. Mix the breadcrumbs with the melted butter and sprinkle over the top of the casserole, along with the remaining cheddar cheese and Parmesan.

  7. Place back in the oven uncovered, until cheeses have melted and everything is warmed through.

  8. Let stand for a few minutes, eat, and enjoy!
